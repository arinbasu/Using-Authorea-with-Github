While prevalence provides you an idea of the probability or the likelihood of a disease, incidence provides you with the idea as to how fast the disease is developing, or an idea as to the hazard associated with the disease. The word hazard here indicates the chance that someone who is disease free up till now will develop the disease or the health condition under study. For this, we need to start with individuals who are free from the disease under study and a sense of time through which they are followed. This is the concept of person-time.
